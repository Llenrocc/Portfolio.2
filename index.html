ORIGIN MIND

• Design ReDefined •

My name's Nathan, and I'm a UI / UX Designer based in Toronto.

Hailing from Guatemala City, I was adopted when I was a year and a half old, coming to Canada to reside in Waterloo, ON. We then moved and I spent most of my life growing up in Listowel (Letterkenny). A small hockey town, I spent most of my time at the rink or in my friends basement listening to / producing music.

After highschool I attended Niagara College for the Film & Acting program. Most of our time was spent in front of the camera however we did have ample classes behind the camera as well. This included recording video & sound, lighting, & editing. From there I then moved to Toronto to attend the Trebas Institute to continue my studies in their film program.
