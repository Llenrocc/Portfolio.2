ORIGIN MIND

• Design ReDefined •

My name's Nathan, and I'm a UI / UX Designer based in Toronto.

Hailing from Guatemala City, I was adopted when I was one and a half and brought to Canada. Originally landing in Waterloo, my family eventually made the decision to move to Listowel, better known as Letterkenny. A small hockey town, I spent most of my time growing up at the rink or in my friends' basement listening to and producing music.

Having a love for stage & film production, I attended Niagara College for their Film & Acting program to hone my craft. Upon graduating, I made the decision to move to Toronto and continue my film studies by completing a post graduate program at Trebas Institute. Fresh out of school I began working at a sports bar where I learned the value of creating positive customer experiences. At this time I started exploring my interest in photography, and I eventually took a job at a media/project management company called Multivista. The company offered photo, video, matterport scanning, webcams & 360 GoPro to record and document various stages of construction projects to be mapped against the clients’ blueprints. A lot of this work was spent in the field, meeting with clients & site supers to discuss their project, their requirements, areas of importance, demonstrate the apps capabilities & schedule upcoming visits.

Fast track to 2020, the company was affected among many others due to Covid-19. This turned out to be a blessing in disguise as I took it as an opportunity to go back to school. I enrolled in the Full Stack Web Development bootcamp at U of T where I was first introduced to UI / UX Design. Learning about design techniques, wireframing & development principles is what kick started my journey into UI / UX. After the bootcamp I took 2 courses on Udemy covering Sketch & Figma. I really enjoy the creative elements of developing a website or application. This includes: colour schemes, typography, wireframes, layout, logo, as well as overall app / page function. I created Origin Mind as a means to brand myself & any work I do for freelance projects. I am looking forward to joining a team I can learn from, collaborate & build with while designing for interesting and exciting projects.
